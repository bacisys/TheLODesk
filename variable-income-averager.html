Salary & hourly base income

TypeTreatment
SalaryCurrent annualized salary ÷ 12. No averaging. A raise within 12 months or under a year at the rate may require the prior rate or an average.
Hourly — full-timeAverage hours/week (YTD base ÷ rate ÷ weeks) at or near the full-time standard → stable base = rate × full-time hrs × 52 ÷ 12. Hours above full-time are overtime, evaluated separately.
Hourly — fluctuatingAverage hours/week below the full-time standard → income converts to variable and runs through the selected program's averaging. Freddie keys fluctuating hourly to a 1-year basis.
