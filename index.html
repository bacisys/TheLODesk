About

Why The Desk exists

Most calculator sites are built for borrowers. The math gets simplified, the edge cases get smoothed over, and the result is a number you can't actually use on a deal. The Desk is built the other way around: for LOs, by an LO. Pro-language, full math exposed, edge cases honored. Designed to live in your bookmarks bar.

If you find a tool that's wrong, missing, or that needs an option I haven't thought of, tell me. This list grows from real deals, not from a product roadmap.
